How do we Safeguard your personal information?

We don't suggest you enter your personal information.

Should you choose at your discretion to enter personal information within the Notes of the App or any information it will be encrypted.

  • Note Vault uses an AES Secret key encrypted with RSA and RSA key is stored in KeyStore, this applies to all Note Titles and Note Body Text
  • Note Body Text is further encrypted with an additional AES with PKCS7 padding and signed with SHA256 and RSA
